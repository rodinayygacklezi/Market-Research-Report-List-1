<p><strong>The Pool Salt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rock Salt</li><li>Solar Salt</li><li>Evap Salt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pool salt market consists of three main types: rock salt, solar salt, and evaporated salt. Rock salt is mined from underground deposits and often contains impurities, making it less expensive. Solar salt is produced by evaporating seawater in shallow ponds, resulting in a purer product suitable for pools. Evaporated salt is generated through the evaporation of brine solution, yielding the highest purity level. Each type has distinct qualities, catering to various pool maintenance needs and preferences.</p></p>
