<p><strong>The Biliary Stent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal Stents</li><li>Polymer Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biliary stent market is primarily categorized into two types: metal stents and polymer stents. Metal stents, often made of stainless steel or nitinol, are durable and provide long-term patency, making them suitable for patients with complex biliary obstructions. In contrast, polymer stents are typically temporary and biodegradable, offering flexibility and ease of placement; they are beneficial in less severe obstructions or for short-term use. Each type serves distinct clinical needs based on the duration and nature of biliary obstruction.</p></p>
