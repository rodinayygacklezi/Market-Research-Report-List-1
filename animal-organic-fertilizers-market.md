<p><p>The animal organic fertilizers market is segmented into dry and liquid types. Dry fertilizers, typically in granule or powder form, are easier to store and apply, providing concentrated nutrients. They are favored for their slow-release capabilities, enhancing soil structure and health. Liquid fertilizers, on the other hand, are more readily absorbed by plants and are often used for foliar applications. They provide quick nutrient access and can be applied through irrigation systems, making them suitable for various agricultural practices.</p></p>
