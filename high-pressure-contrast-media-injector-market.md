<p><ul><li>Single Head</li><li>Dual Head</li><li>Syringeless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Pressure Contrast Media Injector market is classified into three primary types: single head, dual head, and syringeless injectors. Single head injectors are designed for straightforward applications, delivering contrast media through a single syringe. Dual head injectors facilitate simultaneous reservoir use, allowing for increased efficiency and rapid delivery, catering to complex imaging procedures. Syringeless injectors utilize a cartridge system, offering quick, automated contrast media administration without traditional syringes, enhancing patient safety and operational efficiency in medical imaging settings.</p></p>
