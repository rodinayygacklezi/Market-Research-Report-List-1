<p><strong>The High Pressure Reverse Osmosis Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Pressure Type</li><li>External Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Pressure Reverse Osmosis Membrane market consists of two main types: internal pressure type and external pressure type. Internal pressure type membranes operate by utilizing pressure generated within the membrane module, allowing water to permeate through the filtration layer effectively. In contrast, external pressure type membranes rely on an external pressure source to drive water through the membrane, often providing better performance for high salinity feed waters. Both types are essential for various industrial water purification applications.</p></p>
