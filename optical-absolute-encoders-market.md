<p><strong>The Optical Absolute Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Singleturn Encoder</li><li>Multiturn Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical absolute encoders market is primarily divided into two types: singleturn and multiturn encoders. Singleturn encoders provide position data across one full rotation, offering high precision and reliability for applications requiring limited rotational movement. In contrast, multiturn encoders capture the position over multiple rotations, using additional mechanisms to track turns, making them ideal for environments where extensive movement is common. Both types are widely used in industrial automation, robotics, and motion control systems for accurate position sensing.</p></p>
