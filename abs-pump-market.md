<p><p>The ABS pump market is divided into two main types: Electronic ABS Pump and Mechanical ABS Pump. Electronic ABS pumps utilize advanced sensors and control units to monitor wheel speed and modulate braking pressure, offering precise and adaptive braking performance. Mechanical ABS pumps rely on hydraulic systems and mechanical components to achieve similar functionality but with less precision. Both types enhance vehicle safety by preventing wheel lock-up, but electronic systems are more advanced, efficient, and widely adopted in modern vehicles.</p></p>
