<p><p>The submerged arc furnace (SAF) market consists mainly of two types: Direct Current (DC) and Alternating Current (AC) submerged arc furnaces. DC SAFs are known for their efficiency and ability to process high-grade materials, offering better energy consumption and improved metal recovery. In contrast, AC SAFs are typically used for larger production volumes and can handle a wider range of raw materials. Both technologies contribute to the metallurgical industry's capabilities in producing ferroalloys and non-ferrous metals.</p></p>
