<p><p>The medical imaging phantoms market includes various types used for quality assurance and training in imaging modalities. X-ray/fluoroscopy phantoms simulate human tissue for calibration and validation of imaging systems. Ultrasound phantoms assess image quality and Doppler measurement accuracy. CT phantoms evaluate spatial resolution and dose distribution. MRI phantoms ensure signal consistency and image fidelity. Nuclear imaging phantoms are designed for assessing radionuclide distribution and accuracy. Additionally, "Others" encompass specialized phantoms for advanced imaging techniques and research purposes.</p></p>
