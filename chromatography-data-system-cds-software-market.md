<p><p>Chromatography Data System (CDS) software is categorized into two-dimensional and three-dimensional markets based on its analytical capabilities. Two-dimensional CDS enables users to visualize and analyze data in two axes, typically separating different chemical entities on a single graphic interface, simplifying complex data interpretation. In contrast, three-dimensional CDS provides an enhanced visualization by adding a third axis, allowing for more sophisticated analysis, such as simultaneous assessment of multiple parameters. Both types enhance data management and decision-making in chromatographic studies.</p></p>
