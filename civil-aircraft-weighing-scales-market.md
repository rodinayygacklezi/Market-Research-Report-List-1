<p><p>The civil aircraft weighing scales market consists primarily of two types: platform scales and floor-standing scales. Platform scales are designed for larger aircraft, utilizing a flat surface to accommodate the weight of the aircraft during measurement. They provide stability and accuracy for heavier loads. In contrast, floor-standing scales are typically used for smaller, lighter aircraft, offering a compact and portable solution for weighing. Both types ensure precise weight measurements vital for safety and regulatory compliance in aviation operations.</p></p>
