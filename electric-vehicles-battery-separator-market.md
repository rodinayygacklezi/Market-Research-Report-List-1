<p><p>The Electric Vehicle (EV) battery separator market includes various types of separators based on their structure and materials. Single-layer separators, such as polypropylene (PP) and polyethylene (PE), provide basic functionality for ion transport. Bilayer separators, like PP/PE and PP/PP, enhance performance by combining different materials, improving thermal stability and safety. Trilayer separators, incorporating a combination of PP and PE, offer advanced features, including superior electrolyte retention and mechanical strength, crucial for high-performance EV batteries.</p></p>
