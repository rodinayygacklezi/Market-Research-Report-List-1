<p><strong>The Electric Fire Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Single Stage Fire Pump</li><li>Electric Multi Stage Fire Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric fire pump market is primarily segmented into two types: electric single-stage fire pumps and electric multi-stage fire pumps. Electric single-stage fire pumps are designed for low to moderate pressure applications, using a single impeller to deliver water efficiently. In contrast, electric multi-stage fire pumps use multiple impellers to generate higher pressures, making them suitable for high-rise buildings and industrial applications. Both types play critical roles in fire protection systems, ensuring reliable water supply during emergencies.</p></p>
