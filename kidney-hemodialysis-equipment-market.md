<p><strong>The Kidney Hemodialysis Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Pump</li><li>Double Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kidney hemodialysis equipment market is categorized into single pump and double pump systems. Single pump systems utilize one pump for blood circulation and dialysate flow, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. Double pump systems, on the other hand, employ two pumps to independently manage blood and dialysate, allowing for enhanced control over the dialysis process, improved efficiency, and better customization of treatment parameters. These configurations cater to different clinical needs and patient preferences in kidney care.</p></p>
