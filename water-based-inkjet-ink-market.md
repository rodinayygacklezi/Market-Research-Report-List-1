<p><strong>The Water-Based Inkjet Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pigment Ink</li><li>Dye Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water-based inkjet ink market primarily features two types: pigment ink and dye ink. Pigment inks consist of tiny solid particles that provide excellent durability, lightfastness, and water resistance, making them suitable for professional applications and archival printing. In contrast, dye inks are composed of colorants dissolved in liquid, which offer vibrant colors and smooth gradients but may fade over time and have less water resistance. Both types cater to different printing needs, from high-quality graphics to everyday documents.</p></p>
