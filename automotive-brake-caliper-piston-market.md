<p><p>The automotive brake caliper piston market is categorized into two main types: disc brake caliper pistons and drum brake caliper pistons. Disc brake caliper pistons are utilized in disc brake systems, providing efficient clamping force to enhance braking performance and heat dissipation. In contrast, drum brake caliper pistons are part of drum brake systems, which generate braking force by pressing brake shoes against the drum. Each type serves distinct vehicle braking applications, reflecting the diverse needs of the automotive industry.</p></p>
