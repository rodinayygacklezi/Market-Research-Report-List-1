<p><p>Coated sodium percarbonate is a chemical compound widely used as an eco-friendly bleaching and cleaning agent. It is a stable, solid form of hydrogen peroxide combined with sodium carbonate, coated to enhance its shelf life and performance. It is commonly used in detergents, laundry products, and industrial cleaning applications due to its non-toxic and biodegradable properties. The coating ensures controlled release and improved stability, making it suitable for various end-use industries.</p><p>The Coated Sodium Percarbonate Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.7% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand for environmentally friendly cleaning products, rising awareness of sustainable practices, and stringent regulations on chemical usage. The laundry and detergent industry remains the largest consumer, with growing adoption in household and industrial cleaning applications. Additionally, the market is witnessing a surge in demand from the healthcare and water treatment sectors due to its disinfectant properties.</p><p>Recent trends include the development of advanced coating technologies to improve product efficiency and shelf life. Manufacturers are also focusing on expanding production capacities and entering emerging markets to capitalize on growing demand.
