<p><strong>The Chlor-alkali Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Membrane Method</li><li>Diaphragm Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chlor-alkali equipment market is primarily divided into two main types: the Ion Exchange Membrane Method and the Diaphragm Method. The Ion Exchange Membrane Method uses selective membranes to efficiently separate chlorine and caustic soda, resulting in high purity products and lower energy consumption. In contrast, the Diaphragm Method employs a porous diaphragm to separate the electrolytic cells, producing chlorine and sodium hydroxide, but often with lower purity and higher energy costs. Both methods cater to distinct industrial needs and environmental standards.</p></p>
