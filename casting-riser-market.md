<p><strong>The Casting Riser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Type</li><li>Blind Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The casting riser market is segmented into Open Type and Blind Type risers. Open Type risers feature a direct opening to the atmosphere, allowing for easy access for molten metal and facilitating gas escape during the casting process. In contrast, Blind Type risers are sealed, preventing gas escape and promoting more controlled metal flow. Both types cater to different casting applications, influencing factors such as material quality, production efficiency, and the complexity of designs in foundry operations.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Casting Riser Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exothermic Riser</li><li>Insulating Riser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The casting riser market, encompassing exothermic and insulating risers, plays a crucial role in metal casting processes. Exothermic risers optimize heat retention by generating additional thermal energy, ensuring proper solidification of castings and minimizing defects. Insulating risers, on the other hand, provide thermal insulation, slowing down heat loss and enhancing the quality of the final product. Both types are essential for improving casting yields, reducing waste, and enabling the production of complex shapes with superior mechanical properties.</p></p>
