<p><p>Carpet adhesives are essential for securing carpets to floors, with various types catering to different needs. **Epoxy adhesives** offer strong, durable bonds, ideal for heavy-duty applications. **Urethane adhesives** provide flexibility and moisture resistance, suitable for areas prone to movement or dampness. **Acrylic adhesives** are water-based, eco-friendly, and easy to clean, perfect for residential use. **Vinyl adhesives** are versatile, offering strong adhesion for both carpets and vinyl flooring. **Other adhesives** include specialized formulations for unique requirements, such as low-VOC or high-temperature resistance. Each type serves specific installation and environmental conditions.</p></p>
