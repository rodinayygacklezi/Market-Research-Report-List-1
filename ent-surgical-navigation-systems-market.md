<p><strong>The ENT Surgical Navigation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Type</li><li>Electromagnetic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ENT surgical navigation systems can be classified into two main types: optical and electromagnetic. </p><p>Optical systems use camera-based technology to track surgical instruments and patient anatomy with high precision by capturing light signals, providing real-time feedback and enhancing visualization. </p><p>Conversely, electromagnetic systems utilize magnetic fields to determine the position and orientation of instruments relative to the patient's anatomy, allowing for accurate navigation even in various spatial orientations. Both types improve surgical outcomes and efficiency in ENT procedures.</p></p>
