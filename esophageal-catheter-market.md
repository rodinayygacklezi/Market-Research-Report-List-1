<p><p>The esophageal catheter market is diverse, featuring several types designed for specific medical applications. Balloon dilation catheters are used to widen narrowed areas of the esophagus, facilitating easier passage of food and medications. Irrigation catheters help in cleaning and flushing out the esophagus, often used in procedures for aspiration or infection control. Pressure monitoring catheters measure esophageal pressure, crucial for diagnosing motility disorders and gastroesophageal reflux, enhancing patient care through precise diagnostics and treatment options.</p></p>
