<p><ul><li>Differential Pressure Self-operated Balancing Valve</li><li>Static</li><li>Dynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-operated balancing valve market includes three main types: differential pressure, static, and dynamic valves. Differential pressure self-operated balancing valves adjust flow based on pressure fluctuations, maintaining consistent flow rates in varying conditions. Static self-operated balancing valves maintain flow without external energy input, relying solely on system pressure; these are typically simpler in design. Dynamic self-operated balancing valves actively respond to changing system conditions, ensuring optimal flow distribution and energy efficiency, making them suitable for complex systems.</p></p>
