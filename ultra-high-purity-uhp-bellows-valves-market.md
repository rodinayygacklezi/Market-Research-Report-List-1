<p><p>The Ultra High Purity (UHP) Bellows Valves market is segmented into two main types: pneumatic and manual bellows valves. Pneumatic bellows valves utilize compressed air or gas for actuation, offering precise control and rapid operation, making them suitable for automated systems in semiconductor and pharmaceutical applications. On the other hand, manual bellows valves are operated by hand, providing simplicity and reliability for applications where automation is not critical. Both types are essential for maintaining the integrity of ultra-pure processes.</p></p>
