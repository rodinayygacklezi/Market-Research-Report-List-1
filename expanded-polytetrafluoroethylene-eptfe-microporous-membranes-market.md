<p><strong>The Expanded Polytetrafluoroethylene (ePTFE) Microporous Membranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Micron</li><li>Submicron</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ePTFE microporous membranes market is divided into two main types: micron and submicron. Micron membranes are characterized by larger pore sizes typically ranging from 0.1 to 10 micrometers, making them suitable for applications like filtration of larger particles and liquids. In contrast, submicron membranes feature smaller pore sizes, generally below 0.1 micrometers, allowing them to filter finer particles and aerosols, thus serving critical roles in medical, pharmaceutical, and environmental applications where high precision is essential.</p></p>
