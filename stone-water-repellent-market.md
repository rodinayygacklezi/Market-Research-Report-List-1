<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Stone Water Repellent market features two primary types: solvent-based and water-based formulations. Solvent-based repellents generally offer stronger penetration and longer-lasting protection against water and stains, making them ideal for porous stone surfaces. However, they may emit strong odors and require careful handling. In contrast, water-based repellents are more environmentally friendly, easier to apply, and safer for indoor use, though they may provide slightly less durability. Both types are designed to enhance the longevity and appearance of stone surfaces.</p></p>
