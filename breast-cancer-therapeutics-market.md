<p><p>The breast cancer therapeutics market comprises various treatment types targeting different cancer mechanisms. Mitotic inhibitors disrupt cell division, preventing cancer cell proliferation. Anti-metabolites interfere with DNA synthesis, hindering cancer growth. Hormone receptor therapies target hormone-sensitive tumors by blocking hormone actions. Aromatase inhibitors reduce estrogen production, slowing tumor growth in postmenopausal women. HER2 inhibitors specifically target the HER2 protein, effective in aggressive breast cancer types. Together, these therapies offer a comprehensive approach to managing breast cancer.</p></p>
