<p><p>The bamboo fibers market is segmented into natural and chemical bamboo fibers. Natural bamboo fiber is derived from the mechanical processing of bamboo, preserving its eco-friendly characteristics and maintaining the fiber's natural properties, making it ideal for textiles and sustainable products. In contrast, chemical bamboo fiber is produced through a chemical process that breaks down bamboo into pulp, often resulting in a more versatile yet less environmentally friendly product. Both types cater to diverse industries, including textiles, home goods, and eco-friendly products.</p></p>
