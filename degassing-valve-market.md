<p><strong>The Degassing Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Filter Integrated Valves</li><li>Non-Filter Integrated Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The degassing valve market is categorized into two main types: filter integrated valves and non-filter integrated valves. Filter integrated valves incorporate a filtration system to remove particulates and impurities from gasses, ensuring cleaner output and enhanced performance in applications. In contrast, non-filter integrated valves focus on the degassing process itself without additional filtration, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. Both types serve critical roles in industries requiring efficient gas management, such as chemical processing and manufacturing.</p></p>
