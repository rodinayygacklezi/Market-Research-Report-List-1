<p><p>Aramid fibers are high-performance synthetic fibers known for their exceptional strength and thermal stability. The market is primarily divided into two types: para-aramid fibers and meta-aramid fibers. Para-aramid fibers, such as Kevlar, are renowned for their tensile strength, making them ideal for protective gear and aerospace applications. In contrast, meta-aramid fibers, like Nomex, are valued for their heat resistance and are commonly used in fire-resistant clothing and electrical insulation. Both types serve diverse industries, including defense, automotive, and textiles.</p></p>
