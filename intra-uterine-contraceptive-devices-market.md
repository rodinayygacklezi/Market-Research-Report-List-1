<p><strong>The Intra Uterine Contraceptive Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Copper IUCD</li><li>Hormonal IUCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intrauterine contraceptive devices (IUCD) market is primarily divided into two types: copper IUCDs and hormonal IUCDs. Copper IUCDs utilize copper that disrupts sperm motility and creates a toxic environment for sperm, providing long-term contraceptive effects without hormones. In contrast, hormonal IUCDs release progestin, thickening cervical mucus and thinning uterine lining to prevent fertilization and implantation. Both types offer effective, long-lasting contraception options, catering to different preferences and health considerations among individuals seeking family planning solutions.</p></p>
