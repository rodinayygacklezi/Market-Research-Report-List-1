<p><strong>The Air Tightness Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential Pressure Type</li><li>Flow Type</li><li>Gauge Pressure Type</li><li>Others Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air tightness testers market is segmented into different types based on their measurement methods. Differential pressure type testers measure the pressure difference between two sides of a building envelope to assess air leakage. Flow type testers calculate airflow through a known opening to determine tightness. Gauge pressure type testers use a pressure gauge to measure air pressure within an enclosure. Other types include devices using advanced technologies like infrared thermography and tracer gas analysis, expanding the market's scope for various applications.</p></p>
