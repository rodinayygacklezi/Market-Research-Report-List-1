<p><ul><li>System Column Formwork</li><li>Horizontal Formwork</li><li>Slipform</li><li>Vertical Formwork</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete formwork market encompasses various systems designed for efficient concrete placement and structural support. System column formwork is used for vertical columns, ensuring precise dimensions. Horizontal formwork supports slabs and ceilings, facilitating large surface areas. Slipform is a continuous pouring method ideal for structures like walls and towers, allowing for seamless construction. Vertical formwork focuses on walls and vertical surfaces, providing stability and shape during the curing process. Each type offers distinct advantages based on project requirements.</p></p>
