<p><strong>The Fountain Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Submersible Type</li><li>In-line Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fountain pumps market primarily consists of two types: submersible and in-line pumps. Submersible pumps are designed to operate underwater, providing efficient water circulation and are ideal for decorative fountains or ponds. In contrast, in-line pumps are installed above water level and are connected directly to the plumbing system, making them suitable for larger fountains and landscaping features. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, emphasizing efficiency, durability, and ease of maintenance in various applications.</p></p>
