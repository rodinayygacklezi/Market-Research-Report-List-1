<p><p>CNC rotary surface grinders are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their design and functionality. Vertical grinders feature a vertical spindle orientation, allowing for efficient grinding of workpieces at various angles and improving chip clearance. In contrast, horizontal grinders position the spindle horizontally, facilitating a broader range of applications and larger workpiece handling. Both types enhance precision and productivity in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, catering to specific machining needs and preferences.</p></p>
