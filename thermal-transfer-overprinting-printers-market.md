<p><ul><li>Direct Thermal</li><li>Thermal Wax Transfer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal Transfer Overprinting (TTO) printers utilize two main printing methods: direct thermal and thermal wax transfer. Direct thermal printing uses heat-sensitive media that turns dark when heated, making it ideal for short-term applications like labels. In contrast, thermal wax transfer employs a ribbon where heat transfers wax ink onto the substrate, providing high durability and longevity for printed images. Both technologies offer advantages in speed, efficiency, and print quality, catering to various industry needs such as packaging and labeling.</p></p>
