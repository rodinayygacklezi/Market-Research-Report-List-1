<p><p>Weather forecasting systems encompass various instruments used to monitor atmospheric conditions. Barometers measure atmospheric pressure, while anemometers gauge wind speed and direction. Hygrometers assess humidity levels, and rain gauges quantify precipitation. Thermometers track temperature variations, while sling psychrometers evaluate relative humidity using dry and wet bulb temperatures. Weather balloons collect data on temperature, humidity, and pressure at different altitudes. Together, these instruments play a critical role in enhancing weather predictions, benefiting sectors like agriculture, aviation, and disaster management.</p></p>
