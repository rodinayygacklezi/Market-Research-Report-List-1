<p><p>Data center transfer switches and switchgears are essential for maintaining power reliability and uptime. The market includes Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS), which automatically switch to a backup power source during outages, ensuring continuous power supply. Static Transfer Switches (STS) offer fast transfer capabilities between multiple power sources without mechanical moving parts, enhancing responsiveness and reducing downtime. Both types are crucial for data centers, as they enhance power management, increase redundancy, and ensure operational continuity in critical environments.</p></p>
