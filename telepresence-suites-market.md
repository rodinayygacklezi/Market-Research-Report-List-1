<p><p>Telepresence suites are advanced communication systems that enable immersive virtual meetings. The static telepresence market includes fixed installations in dedicated rooms, offering high-quality video and audio for in-person-like interactions. In contrast, remote telepresence systems are more flexible, allowing users to connect from various locations through mobile devices or laptops. Both types enhance collaboration and reduce travel costs but differ in their setups—static systems prioritize a consistent experience in a specific location, while remote systems focus on accessibility and mobility.</p></p>
