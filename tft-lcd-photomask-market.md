<p><p>The TFT-LCD photomask market consists of two primary types: soda-lime and synthetic quartz. Soda-lime photomasks are cost-effective and widely utilized for less demanding applications due to their favorable optical properties and availability. In contrast, synthetic quartz photomasks offer superior thermal stability and lower light absorption, making them ideal for high-resolution displays and advanced applications. The differentiation in materials reflects the varying performance requirements within the TFT-LCD manufacturing process, catering to diverse customer needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
