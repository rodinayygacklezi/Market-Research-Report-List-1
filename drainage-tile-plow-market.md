<p><p>The drainage tile plow market is primarily divided into two types: pull type and mounted type. Pull type plows are towed by a tractor, allowing for deeper and wider trenching, making them suitable for larger fields. In contrast, mounted type plows are attached directly to the tractor, providing better maneuverability and control in smaller or more complex landscapes. Both types serve the critical function of installing drainage tiles to improve soil drainage and enhance agricultural productivity.</p></p>
