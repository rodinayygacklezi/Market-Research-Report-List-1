<p><p>The shower glass door market is primarily divided into two types: frameless and framed. In contrast, framed shower doors have an aluminum or metal frame that provides structural support, enhancing durability and often reducing costs. These options cater to different consumer preferences, with frameless doors appealing to those prioritizing design, while framed doors may attract budget-conscious buyers or those seeking reinforced support.</p></p>
