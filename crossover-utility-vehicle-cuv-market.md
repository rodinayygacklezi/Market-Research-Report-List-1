<p><p>A Crossover Utility Vehicle (CUV) is a type of vehicle that combines the features of a traditional SUV with those of a passenger car. They typically provide ample interior space, higher ground clearance, and versatile cargo options, making them popular among families and urban drivers.</p><p>The Crossover Utility Vehicle (CUV) market has seen significant growth due to increasing consumer preference for vehicles that balance practicality, comfort, and efficiency. Additionally, the demand for luxury CUVs is rising, with premium brands expanding their offerings to capture a larger share of the market.
