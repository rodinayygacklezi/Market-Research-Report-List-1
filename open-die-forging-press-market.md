<p><p>The open die forging press market is categorized into three main types: push-down forging presses, pull-down type forging presses, and others. Push-down forging presses apply pressure from above, allowing for efficient shaping of large metal pieces. Pull-down type forging presses exert force from below, offering enhanced control for intricate shapes. Meanwhile, the "others" category encompasses various specialized presses that cater to specific forging processes, materials, and industry needs, thus broadening the versatility and application of open die forging technologies.</p></p>
