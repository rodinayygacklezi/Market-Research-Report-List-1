Short-range forecasting typically covers up to 48 hours and focuses on immediate weather conditions, often used for daily planning. Medium-range forecasting extends from 3 to 10 days, providing insights for weekly events and travel. Long-range forecasting, lasting from weeks to months, offers seasonal trends and climatic predictions, aiding in agriculture and disaster preparedness. Each type serves distinct needs for various sectors and audiences.</p></p>
