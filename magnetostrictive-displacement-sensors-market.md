<p><p>Magnetostrictive displacement sensors are categorized by their ingress protection (IP) ratings, which indicate their suitability for various environmental conditions. IP67 sensors are dust-tight and can withstand immersion in water up to 1 meter for 30 minutes. IP68 sensors offer more robust protection, suitable for continuous submersion beyond 1 meter. IP69 sensors are designed for high-pressure and high-temperature wash-down applications. "Others" may include sensors with different or specialized ratings for unique industrial needs, expanding their application in diverse environments.</p></p>
