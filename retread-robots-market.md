<p><p>The retread robots market categories include articulated, Cartesian, cylindrical, polar, SCARA, and delta robots. Articulated robots offer flexibility with rotating joints, ideal for complex tasks. Cartesian robots use linear movements along orthogonal axes, providing simplicity and precision. Cylindrical robots operate in a cylindrical work envelope, combining rotary and linear motions. Polar robots have a spherical work area, useful for tasks requiring wide reach. SCARA robots excel in horizontal movement, suitable for assembly, while delta robots are fast and precise for high-speed pick and place operations.</p></p>
