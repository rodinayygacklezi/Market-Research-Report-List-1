<p><p>The technical films market is categorized into degradable and non-degradable films. Degradable films are designed to break down over time through environmental exposure, making them more environmentally friendly and suitable for applications like packaging and single-use items. Non-degradable films, on the other hand, possess durability and longevity, often used in industrial applications, protective coverings, and agricultural films. While degradable films address sustainability concerns, non-degradable films are favored for their strength and extended usability in various sectors.</p></p>
