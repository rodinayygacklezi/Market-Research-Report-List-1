<p><p>The Video Measuring Machines (VMM) market can be categorized into two primary types: manual and automatic. In contrast, automatic video measuring machines streamline the measurement process with pre-programmed routines, providing higher precision and efficiency. These automated systems enhance productivity, reduce human error, and are ideal for high-volume settings where consistent accuracy is crucial.</p></p>
