<p><p>The ultralight helicopters market is categorized primarily into two types: piston engine and turboshaft engine helicopters. Piston engine ultralight helicopters are typically lighter, more cost-effective, and simpler to maintain, making them suitable for recreational flying and training purposes. In contrast, turboshaft ultralight helicopters offer higher power-to-weight ratios, allowing for better performance and efficiency, particularly in commercial applications.
