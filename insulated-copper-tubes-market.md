<p><p>The insulated copper tubes market can be segmented into two main types: extruded copper tubes and drawn copper tubes. Extruded copper tubes are produced by forcing molten copper through a die, resulting in uniform thickness and various shapes suited for different applications. In contrast, drawn copper tubes are created by drawing solid copper through a die to achieve desired dimensions and improved mechanical properties. Both types are essential in industries like HVAC, refrigeration, and plumbing, where thermal efficiency and corrosion resistance are crucial.</p></p>
