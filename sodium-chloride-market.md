<p><p>The sodium chloride market comprises three main types: Solar Salt, Rock Salt, and Vacuum Salt. Solar Salt is produced by evaporating seawater or brine in solar ponds, commonly used in water softening and de-icing. Rock Salt is mined from underground deposits and is widely used for road de-icing and industrial applications. Vacuum Salt is refined through evaporation under vacuum, resulting in high purity, making it suitable for food, pharmaceuticals, and chemical industries. Each type serves distinct applications based on purity and production methods.</p></p>
