<p><p>FFP masks are categorized into three classes based on filtration efficiency. FFP1 masks filter at least 80% of airborne particles and are suitable for low-risk environments. FFP2 masks provide a higher filtration level, capturing at least 94% of particles, making them ideal for moderate-risk situations. FFP3 masks offer the highest protection, filtering at least 99% of particles and are used in high-risk environments, such as healthcare settings. Each type serves distinct needs depending on the level of exposure to harmful substances.</p></p>
