<p><strong>The CO2 Medical Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fractional CO2 Laser</li><li>Completely Ablative CO2 Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CO2 medical lasers market comprises two primary types: fractional CO2 lasers and completely ablative CO2 lasers. Fractional CO2 lasers are designed to target specific areas of skin, promoting healing while minimizing damage to surrounding tissues, making them ideal for treating scars and wrinkles. In contrast, completely ablative CO2 lasers remove the outer layer of skin entirely, providing more dramatic results for deeper skin concerns but requiring longer recovery times. Both types are popular in aesthetic and dermatological treatments.</p></p>
