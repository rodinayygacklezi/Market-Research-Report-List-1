<p><p>The screwing robot market comprises two primary types: Air Suction Type and Air Blow Type. The Air Suction Type utilizes vacuum technology to securely grip and manipulate screws during assembly, ensuring precision and efficiency. In contrast, the Air Blow Type employs a burst of air to propel screws into position, facilitating rapid placements in high-speed production scenarios. Both types enhance automation in manufacturing, improving accuracy and reducing manual labor while catering to various industry needs and assembly line configurations.</p></p>
