<p><p>ToFD (Time of Flight Diffraction) scanners are essential tools in non-destructive testing, particularly in weld inspections. The market is segmented into one-axis and two-axis scanners. One-axis scanners allow movement along a single line, offering a basic but effective means of detecting flaws in welds. In contrast, two-axis scanners enable movement in both horizontal and vertical directions, providing a more comprehensive and detailed examination of complex geometries. This versatility makes two-axis scanners increasingly popular in industries requiring thorough inspection.</p></p>
