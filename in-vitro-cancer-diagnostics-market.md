<p><p>The In-Vitro Cancer Diagnostics market is primarily categorized into three types: instruments, reagents, and kits. Instruments include advanced devices used for analyzing biological samples, such as spectrophotometers and PCR machines. Reagents are chemical substances used in diagnostic tests to detect cancer biomarkers, including antibodies and enzymes. Kits combine various reagents and instruments in a single package for ease of use, streamlining the diagnostic process. Together, these components enhance the accuracy and efficiency of cancer detection and monitoring.</p></p>
