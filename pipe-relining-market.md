<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pipe Relining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cured-in-place</li><li>Pull-in-place</li><li>Pipe Bursting</li><li>Internal Pipe Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipe relining market encompasses various techniques for rehabilitating damaged pipelines. Cured-in-place involves inserting a resin-saturated liner, which is then cured to form a new pipe within the existing one. Pull-in-place uses a flexible liner pulled into place and cured on-site. Pipe bursting replaces old pipes by breaking them apart while simultaneously installing a new pipe. Internal pipe coating applies a protective layer to the interior of existing pipes, enhancing their durability and flow characteristics. Each method offers unique benefits for pipeline maintenance.</p></p>
