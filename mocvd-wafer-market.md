<p><p>The MOCVD wafer market includes various types catering to different high-demand applications. HBT (Heterojunction Bipolar Transistors) excel in high-speed and high-frequency operations, often found in telecommunications. LD (Laser Diodes) are essential for optical applications such as fiber optics and laser-based technologies, while PD (Photodiodes) serve in light detection systems. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing semiconductor technology and performance.</p></p>
