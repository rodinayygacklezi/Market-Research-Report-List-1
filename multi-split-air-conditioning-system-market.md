<p><p>The multi-split air conditioning system market includes two main types: single-zone multi-split and multi-zone multi-split systems. A single-zone multi-split system connects multiple indoor units to a single outdoor unit, ideal for effectively cooling or heating individual spaces in smaller buildings. In contrast, the multi-zone multi-split system accommodates multiple indoor units paired with one outdoor unit for larger buildings or homes, allowing for customized climate control across different rooms or areas, enhancing energy efficiency and comfort.</p></p>
