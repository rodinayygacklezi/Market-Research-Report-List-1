<p><ul><li>IGBT Modules</li><li>IGBT Discretes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PV inverter IGBT market is primarily divided into two types: IGBT modules and IGBT discretes. IGBT modules are integrated packages that combine multiple IGBT chips, offering high efficiency and compact design, making them ideal for high-power applications in photovoltaic systems. In contrast, IGBT discretes are individual chips that provide flexibility in design and scalability for various power ratings. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and efficiency of solar inverters, enabling effective energy conversion and management.</p></p>
