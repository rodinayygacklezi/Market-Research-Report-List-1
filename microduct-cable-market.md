<p><p>The Microduct Cable market includes various types of ducts, such as Thick Walled Ducts (TWD) and Tight Protected Ducts (TPD). TWDs are designed with robust walls to withstand external pressures and environmental challenges, making them ideal for harsh conditions. In contrast, TPDs offer enhanced protection through a tighter design, ensuring optimal safety for fiber cables while minimizing space usage. Both types cater to the growing demand for efficient and durable network solutions in telecommunication and data transmission applications.</p></p>
