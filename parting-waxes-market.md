<p><ul><li>Conventional Parting Wax</li><li>Semi-Permanent Parting Wax</li><li>Silicone-Free Parting Wax</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parting waxes are essential in molding processes, serving as release agents. The conventional parting wax is typically a blend of natural and synthetic waxes, providing a reliable barrier but requiring frequent reapplication. Semi-permanent parting wax offers longer-lasting performance, reducing the need for reapplication and improving mold surface finish. Silicone-free parting waxes cater to specific applications where silicone contamination is a concern, ensuring compatibility with coatings and finishes, while still providing effective release properties. Each type caters to distinct industry needs.</p></p>
