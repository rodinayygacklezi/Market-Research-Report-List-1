<p><p>The plunger sensors market encompasses various types, including 2-wire sensors, 3-wire sensors, and rattle sensors. 2-wire sensors are simple, requiring only two connections for power and signal, making them cost-effective and easy to install. 3-wire sensors, on the other hand, offer enhanced functionality with an additional wire for signal return, allowing for better accuracy and reliability. Rattle sensors are designed to detect vibrations and movements, providing valuable data in applications like industrial automation and safety monitoring.</p></p>
