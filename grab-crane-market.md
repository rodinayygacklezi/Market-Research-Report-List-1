<p><p>The grab crane market comprises three main types: bridge, portal, and folding arm cranes. Bridge cranes operate on elevated rails, providing efficient overhead lifting in industrial settings. Portal cranes, often found in shipping yards, feature a cantilevered design for both mobility and stability, ideal for heavy lifting in outdoor spaces. Folding arm cranes, characterized by their retractable arms, are versatile and space-saving, suitable for various applications such as construction and logistics, allowing flexibility in confined areas.</p></p>
