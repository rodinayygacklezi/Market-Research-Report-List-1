<p><p>Petroleum coke, or petcoke, is a byproduct of oil refining, categorized into various types based on physical and chemical properties. Needle coke, known for its high purity and crystalline structure, is primarily used in electrodes for aluminum and steel production. Shot coke has a more spherical shape and is often employed in power generation. Sponge coke, with its porous structure, is utilized in the manufacture of anodes. Honeycomb coke features a cellular structure, mainly serving in industrial applications and as a carbon source.</p></p>
