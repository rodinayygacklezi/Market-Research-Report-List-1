<p><p>TEM specimen preparation equipment encompasses various tools essential for preparing samples for Transmission Electron Microscopy. The PIPS System (Precision Ion Polishing System) is used for precise thinning and ion milling, while the Dimple Grinder creates a shallow dome on the specimen surface. Disc Punches are employed for cutting circular samples, and Disc Grinders are utilized for planarizing samples to desired thickness. Other equipment may include polishing machines and various milling tools, all contributing to sample quality and imaging accuracy.</p></p>
