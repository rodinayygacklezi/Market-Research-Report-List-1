<p><p>The rechargeable lithium battery market primarily consists of two types: cylindrical and prismatic batteries. Cylindrical batteries are characterized by their round shape, often found in portable electronics and electric vehicles due to their high energy density and robust structure. Prismatic batteries, on the other hand, have a flat, rectangular design, making them space-efficient and suitable for applications like smartphones and laptops. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, balancing factors such as capacity, size, and weight in various applications.</p></p>
