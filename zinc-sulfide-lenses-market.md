<p><ul><li>Spherical Lens</li><li>Aspheric Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Zinc Sulfide lenses market includes two primary types: spherical lenses and aspheric lenses. Spherical lenses have a uniform curvature, making them easier to manufacture and often used in basic optical applications. In contrast, aspheric lenses feature a more complex curvature, allowing for improved optical performance by reducing aberrations and providing better focus. These lenses are favored in high-performance applications such as military optics and infrared systems, where precise imaging and clarity are essential.</p></p>
