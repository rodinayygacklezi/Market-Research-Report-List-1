<p><p>SiC MOSFETs (Silicon Carbide Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors) are classified into distinct market types based on their voltage ratings: 650 V, 1200 V, and 1700 V types. The 650 V type is commonly used in applications requiring moderate power levels, such as power supplies and motor drives. The 1200 V type is suitable for industrial and automotive applications, providing higher efficiency and thermal performance. The 1700 V type targets high-voltage applications, including renewable energy systems and HVDC transmission, while 'Other' encompasses specialized uses or emerging applications.</p></p>
