<p><p>The inflatable dome market is categorized into two primary types: transparent and intransparent. Transparent domes are made from see-through materials, allowing natural light to enter and providing an unobstructed view of surroundings, making them ideal for events or exhibitions. In contrast, intransparent domes prioritize privacy and temperature control, often featuring solid-colored or opaque materials. They are commonly used for specialized applications like sports facilities or temporary shelters, offering enhanced insulation and visual seclusion while maintaining structural integrity.</p></p>
