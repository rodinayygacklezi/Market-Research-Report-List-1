<p><strong>The MEMS Air Velocity Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Air Velocity Sensor</li><li>Ultrasonic Air Velocity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS air velocity sensor market features two primary types: mechanical and ultrasonic air velocity sensors. Mechanical air velocity sensors operate by detecting the airflow impacting a moving element, providing precise measurements of air movement. In contrast, ultrasonic air velocity sensors use sound waves to determine air velocity by measuring the time it takes for ultrasonic signals to travel between transducers across the airflow. Both technologies serve vital roles in HVAC systems, industrial processes, and environmental monitoring, ensuring accurate air velocity measurements.</p></p>
