<p><p>The military infrared sensor chip market is primarily divided into two types: refrigeration type and uncooled type sensors. Refrigeration-type sensors, often utilizing advanced cooling techniques, provide high sensitivity and improved resolution, making them suitable for advanced thermal imaging applications. In contrast, uncooled type sensors operate at ambient temperatures, offering lighter weight, lower power consumption, and reduced overall costs, making them ideal for portable and rapid deployment scenarios in military operations. Each type serves distinct operational needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
