<p><strong>The Digital X-Ray Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray Imaging</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital X-Ray technology encompasses two primary market types: Computed Radiography (CR) and Digital Radiography (DR). CR involves the use of imaging plates that capture X-ray data and are subsequently processed digitally, offering a cost-effective solution for facilities transitioning from traditional film. In contrast, DR utilizes digital detectors to directly convert X-ray photons into electrical signals, providing faster processing times, higher image quality, and real-time viewing. Both technologies enhance diagnostic capabilities while improving workflow efficiency in medical settings.</p></p>
