<p><p>Proportional solenoid valves are critical in medical applications for precise fluid control. Direct acting proportional solenoid valves operate based on the input current, directly controlling flow without a pilot, providing rapid response and compact design. Pilot proportional solenoid valves utilize a smaller pilot valve to control larger main flow, offering efficient operation in complex systems. Both types enable accurate regulation of gases and liquids, enhancing the performance of medical devices such as infusion pumps and ventilators.</p></p>
